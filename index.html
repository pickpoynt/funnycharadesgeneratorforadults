How to Use This Funny Charades Generator for Adults

Welcome to the ultimate funny charades generator for adults! If you're looking to spice up your game night with hilarious, awkward, and slightly naughty prompts, you've come to the right place.

How to Play

  1. Split into Teams: Divide your group into Team A and Team B.
  2. Set the Timer: Choose between a 60-second or 90-second round using the buttons below the card.
  3. Generate a Card: Click the "Next Card" button to reveal a random funny charade.
  4. Act it Out: One player acts out the prompt without speaking while their team guesses.
  5. Score Points: If the team guesses correctly before time runs out, use the + button to add a point!
